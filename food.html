Food - Discover Delicious & Healthy Eating

Fuel your body with nutritious meals and explore the culinary delights of Malaysia!

Did you know? Eating a balanced diet can improve your mood, energy levels, and overall health. If you want to practice a balanced diet, you need to eat according to the food pyramid.

FOOD PYRAMID

The food pyramid is a reference for each individual to control the quantity of each type of food according to the daily serving recommendations.

Level Name Food
Level 1 Carbohydrates Bread,Rice, potatoes, cookies, spaghetti
Level 2 Vitamin Fruits, Vegetables
Level 3 Protien Meat,Fish,Eggs
Level 4 Fat,sugar,salt Sweets, Oils, Processed Foods

Quantity intake

  • Carbohydrates: Eat enough
  • Vitamin: Eat a lot
  • Protien: Eat moderately
  • Fat,sugar,salt: Eat sparingly
